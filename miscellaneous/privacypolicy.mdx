This Privacy Policy describes how ERLCHelper ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and discloses the information we collect from users ("you", "your") of our service. By using our service, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect
We collect the following data in sole purpose to ensure that we are fully functional and running. This information is only used to make sure that we are fully functional.
- Configuration data (role IDs, channel IDs, configuration options selected, module settings)
- User data (username, user ID, avatar)
- Guild data (guiild ID, server name, guild configuration data, server owner)
- Command usage (when they were used, who used them, and what commands were used)

## How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- To provide, maintain, and improve our service.
- To personalize user experiences and interactions within our service.
- To analyze usage patterns and trends to enhance the functionality and performance of our service.
- To communicate with users about updates, announcements, and other service-related information.

## How We Disclose Your Information
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

- We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our service, conducting our business, or servicing you.
- We may disclose your information in response to lawful requests from public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements.
-We may disclose your information if we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, or situations involving potential threats to the safety of any person.

## Data Retention
We retain the information we collect for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

## Your Rights and Choices
You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You may also choose to opt out of certain data collection and processing practices. Please contact us via our [support server](https://discord.gg/8KhR775rxx) to exercise your rights and choices.

Our management team reserves the right to change our polices at any given time with or without any prior notice. Users are responsible for ensuring they are up to date with our policy changes and ensure that they are following our polices at all given times when accessing the service.
